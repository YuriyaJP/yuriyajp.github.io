About Me

What I do: I build monitoring systems and automated detection frameworks that surface critical signals in high-volume data streams. I've analyzed national healthcare datasets spanning 9 years, built NLP pipelines that process thousands of consumer health reports, and designed anomaly detection systems that flag risk patterns before they escalate.

My work focuses on reliability engineering where system performance directly impacts user safety and experience. Whether it's designing observability frameworks for latency-sensitive APIs, implementing automated alerting for production anomalies, or building self-healing infrastructure I solve problems where downtime and degraded performance have immediate consequences.

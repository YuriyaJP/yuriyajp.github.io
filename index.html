About Me

My journey began in psychology and linguistics, studying how humans communicate and behave. I developed my research skills as a behavioural analysis researcher at Chiba University, examining patterns of human behaviour and cognition. I have since transitioned into social data science, analysing the cost-effectiveness of data-driven interventions in social contexts. I work with Python, SQL, Tableau, Excel and Machine Learning tools to extract insights from complex datasets.

Fluent in Japanese (N2), German (C1), English (C1) and Ukrainian (Native).

Goal

Assist students and educators by creating an audio-based PDF reader that reads study materials aloud, supporting inclusive and accessible learning.

Tools Used

Python, VS Code, System Voice APIs

Outcomes

  • Automated reading of PDFs with natural voice output.
  • Improved accessibility for students with reading difficulties.
  • Enhanced learning experience through auditory reinforcement.

Timeframe

2023, Japan

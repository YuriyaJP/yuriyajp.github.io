Generative AI Usage Among Licensed Professionals in Japan

Data Analysis · Tableau · Survey Analytics · Qualitative Synthesis

Overview

This freelance analytics project examines how licensed professionals in Japan (such as lawyers, accountants, and consultants) perceive and use generative AI tools like ChatGPT in their daily work.

The analysis combines structured survey data with free-text responses and was delivered as a multi-page Tableau report designed for non-technical stakeholders.

Key Findings

Adoption Landscape

  • High awareness of generative AI tools
  • ChatGPT is the dominant platform
  • Clear split between active users and hesitant adopters

Primary Use Cases

  • Idea generation and thought structuring
  • Document drafting and summarization
  • Research and analytical support

Perceived Client Value

  • Improved proposal quality and accuracy
  • More time for core client problem-solving
  • Higher-quality information sharing

Barriers to Adoption

  • Difficulty imagining concrete use cases
  • Trust, accuracy, and security concerns
  • Organizational and policy restrictions

Findings are based on self-reported survey responses and qualitative coding of open-ended answers.

Tableau Analysis

A 16-page Tableau report was produced to visualize usage patterns, sentiment, tool adoption, and perceived value across respondent segments.

Methodology

  • Quantitative aggregation of SA / MA responses

Tools Used

Tableau Desktop, Tableau Public, Excel/Google Sheets, statistical analysis, custom visualization design.

Project Overview

This freelance project analyzed generative AI usage among 40 Japanese respondents, combining quantitative survey data and qualitative free-text responses.

Process

  • Collected, cleaned, and standardized Japanese-language survey responses.
  • Produced a 16-page Tableau report visualizing trends and usage patterns.
  • Conducted statistical analysis to identify key behaviors and sentiment themes.

Results

  • Delivered a structured report with clear insights into generative AI adoption and perception.
  • Provided data-backed visuals that strengthened the client's understanding of user knowledge and use of AI tools in everyday life.

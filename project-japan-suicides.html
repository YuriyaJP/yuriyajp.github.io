Public Health Data Engineering Japan Suicide Statistics Pipeline

Scraping, cleaning, and analyzing Japan police suicide statistics to provide interactive visualizations and time-series forecasts at the population level, supporting evidence-based public health insights.

Home

Goal

Build a sustainable, automated pipeline for Japan police suicide statistics, visualize trends with interactive dashboards, and apply time-series modeling to understand population-level patterns over time.

Methods

  • Data engineering: web scraping, cleaning, and building a self-updating pipeline
  • Data visualization: interactive dashboards for trend analysis
  • ML time-series forecasting: regression-based trend modeling for aggregated statistics
  • Economic Impact: estimating lost lifetime tax revenue and productivity to illustrate cost-effectiveness of prevention

Outcome

The pipeline and dashboard provide population-level insights and forecasts. Forecasts highlight trends that can inform preventive strategies and resource planning, helping public health centers target interventions where they are most needed.
